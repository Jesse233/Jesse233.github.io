About me

I am currently a third year Master student, affiliated with the CASIA and SKL-MCCS labs. My advisors are Prof. Weiqun Wang and Prof. Zeng-Guang Hou.

My research focuses on developing computationally efficient machine learning as well as deep learning models and algorithms in computer version, medical image analysis, and Brain-Computer Interface.
