About me

I am currently a first year PhD student, affiliated with the Inria Sophia Antipolis and Epione labs. My advisors are Prof. Nicholas Ayache and Prof. Hervé Delingette.

My research topics include multi-modal image registration, image-guided therapy, medical image analysis, and statistical learning.
